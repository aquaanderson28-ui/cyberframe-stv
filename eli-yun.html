About the Film “Eli & Yun”

Film Description

“Eli & Yun” is a story about two friends. Their bond seemed unbreakable until one choice turned everything upside down. The film shows how friendship can turn into pain, how trust can collapse from a single action, and how difficult it is to understand what matters more — to preserve yourself or to preserve the connection. “Eli & Yun” is a very large film about how dangerous it can be to find yourself in an unfamiliar environment, and how important it is to value what you have.
